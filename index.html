A Brief Timeline

Through seeing the world from many perspectives: from the different cultures to the languages, and even to the flavors, her wide and unique background has become a valuable part of Rissa's outlook, not only in how she interacts with the world, but in how she helps shape it.

  • 1984

    Born in Manila, Philippines

    It was a hot sunny Monday that marked her arrival into the world, in the vibrant city of Manila. This laid the foundation for a life of diverse experiences and global travels, although she would soon leave her birthplace for new destinations.

    1989

    Moved to Singapore / Hong Kong

    Rissa's international journey began in Singapore, but after only a short time quickly relocated again, exposing her to the dynamic cultures of both Singapore and Hong Kong. This period instilled in her an appreciation for multiculturalism and adaptability.

  • 1995

    Moved to Kobe, Japan

    The serene beauty and unique culture of Kobe became Rissa's home for a time. This experience fostered a sense of global citizenship and broadened her perspective.

  • 1996

    Moved to Cincinnati

    A new chapter began as she moved to Cincinnati, Ohio. This marked a transition to a different pace of life and culture as she grew up.

  • 2008

    Moved to NYC

    Post-graduation, the energy and excitement of New York City beckoned, leading Rissa to puruse her career in education there, and experience life in one of the world's most iconic cities. This period was filled with personal and professional growth.

  • 2009

    Settled in Cincinnati

    Returning to Cincinnati after her fiance had completed his Iraq deployment, she married her now-husband, Ronnie, established roots and built a home. This city became a place of stability and community.
